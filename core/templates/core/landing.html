We build AI that stays consistent over time

StableMind is developing a new layer for AI systems that allows them to maintain a stable personality across long-term interactions.

Today’s AI can respond intelligently, but it often lacks a persistent identity — it changes behavior depending on context and loses consistency over time.

We are working on an architecture that separates short-term adaptation from long-term personality evolution, enabling AI systems to behave like stable, coherent entities rather than reactive tools.

Our work is grounded in ongoing research in memory systems, agent behavior, and cognitive modeling.
