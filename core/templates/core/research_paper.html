StableMind: A Two-Timescale Architecture for Persistent Personality in LLM Agents

Hasan Mavlonov, ECUST · Major in Artificial Intelligence

We propose StableMind, a memory- and reflection-based architecture that decouples short-term belief plasticity from long-term trait evolution. Trait evolution occurs only when drift satisfies tri-fold criteria of magnitude, confidence, and observation count — ensuring updates are bounded and stepwise.

Endorsed for arXiv submission · Nuo Chen, NUS — author of "Diversity Collapse in Multi-Agent LLM Systems"
